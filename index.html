VISIT BJELAŠNICA

Years after hosting the Winter Olympics, Bjelašnica is still a hotspot for people of all ages and walks of life to find a bit of fresh air outside of the capital city on one of the most beautiful Bosnian mountains, fun in the snow with the whole family or a group of friends that's just out to have a good time and make memories that will have them coming back for more when the next season starts. Don't worry if you're a beginner or have no equipment - we're more than ready to provide you with professional instructors and top tier equipment for maximum enjoyment! Join them in discovering just why Bjelašnica is the most popular winter holiday destination in Bosnia and Herzegovina, we're expecting you!
